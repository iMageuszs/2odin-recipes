Easy Lasagna

Tim's Easy Lasagna Recipe

Why cook this?

It can be insanely delicious. Level of the tase is relative to the price of the ingredients used.

Here I will list both cheap/expensive ingredients to use.

What tools you will need

Baking dish and aluminum foil, frying pan.

Ingredients

I personally prefer lasagna sheets that do not require cooking before layering. So i will use those in the recipe.

I will put cheaper ingredients first | followed by more expensive ones.

    The list:
  • minced beef | chopped beef
  • onions | white onions
  • tomato paste | tomatoes
  • bell peppers
  • any cheese | fresh mozzarella and parmiggianno
  • spices (nutmeg, black pepper, coriander) | and fresh basil
  • oil with high smoking point
  • salt TT

Let's get cooking!

Before you start: when all mise en place is done, preheat the oven to 180 C.

Also if you are going to buy the baking dish, then take a standart sized lasagna one. Because lasagnya dough sheets usually are standartized in size.

    Mise en Place:

  1. cut onions, chop tomatoes and bell peppers
  2. grate mozarella cheese, or cut, so it's convenient to layer it thinly

    Preparation process:

  1. season meat, add onions and stirfry on high heat until golden. be careful not to overcook it: 3,5/5 coocked
  2. remove meat from pan and add onions, tomatoes, bell peppers. stew until they become one mass. this will be the sauce
    3. now build lasagna:
    • thin layer of sauce on the bottom of the baking dish
    • then lasagna sheet
    • sauce
    • meat
    • mozarella cheese
    • then repeat starting from lasagna sheet
    • and then cover it all with some parmiggiano cheese on top
  4. cover it with aluminum foil and put in oven for 45 minutes or more, depends on the quantity of layers
  5. remove aluminum foil and let crust form for about 15 minutes
  6. after that, take out of the oven, garnish with some basil leaves and let it sit for 10 minutes before cutting
  7. cut, serve, enjoy!

ENJOY!
