Kotlety and Rice

Tim's Kotlety and Rice recipe

Why cook this?

It's cheap, ridiculously easy to cook and surprisingly good.

Can be stored in a ready to cook state in a fridge for some time, like a week and a half or so.

Will also include a russian salad recipe.

What tools you will need

A pot for the rice and a skillet with a lid for kotlety, a container to store leftover kotlety meat.

Ingredients

Technically you can choose what ever ground meat you like, but i prefer my kotlety from beef.

Beef will store better than chicken or pork, so if you are considering having kotlety today and in a week, then choose beef.

I am not taking lamb or horse meat, because it's a budget build...i mean budget food.

    The list:
  • 500gr ground meat
  • 300gr onions
  • rice
  • 1pc tomatoes
  • 2pc cucumbers
  • 50g sour cream
  • sunflower oil
  • spices like corriander, nutmeg, or whatever you have
  • salt and pepper TT

Let's get cooking!

Personally i prefer my kotlety with roughly chooped onions and without eggs or other binding agents,

But if you want to impress yourself, then you can add 1 egg for every 500gr of meat.

    Mise en Place:

  1. chop onions

    Preparation process:

  1. put meat, chopped onions, spices, oil, salt and pepper into a bowl
  2. mix with hands while smashing onions to release juice
  3. start the rice, considering int takes 15 minutes to fry kotlety
  4. and preheat your skillet and add some oil
  5. shape your kotlety, 100-150 gr each
  6. on a hot skillet sear kotlety to promote more taste
  7. then cover skillet with lid and reduce heat to low and let stew for 7-10 mins
  8. meanwile cut tomatoes and cucumbers, add sour cream
  9. now rice should be ready, put some on your plate, put kotlety and pour the liquid from the skillet over the rice
With what it is enjoyed well

A shot of russian vodka right before having a bite of kotlety and covering it up with a bit of salad.
